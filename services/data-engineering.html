Our Data Engineering Workflow

Requirements Gathering

Identify business needs by defining data sources, types, and quality requirements, focusing on desired outcomes to ensure alignment with stakeholder goals.

Data Ingestion

Collect structured and unstructured data from various sources like databases, APIs, and streaming platforms to centralize it for processing.

Data Cleaning

Eliminate duplicates, fill in missing values, and standardize formats to ensure the data is accurate, consistent, and ready for analysis and decision-making.

Data Storage

Store processed data in relational databases, data lakes, or cloud-based solutions to ensure accessibility and scalability.

Transformation and ETL

Convert data into usable formats and perform extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL) into target systems for reporting and analytics.

Data Serving

Deliver data to end users like analysts, ML engineers, and managers, enabling them to generate insights and optimize business processes.
